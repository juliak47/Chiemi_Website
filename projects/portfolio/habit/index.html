7 Habits, How can help teens?

  • Get control of your life
  • Increase your self-confidence
  • Get more done in less time
  • Overcome addiction and self-destractive habits
  • Improve your relationships with people

1     Be Proactive !

You spend too much time worrying about things on which you have no control. Instead, focus on things we can change. And do it proactively, before you are forced to do something about it. Expand the circle of influence.

2     Begin with the End in Mind !

If you don’t know where you want to go, how will you ever get there? Envision your furureusing both logic and imagination. Let it be based on principles, Write down your friends, family, colleagues, community will tell about you in your funeral. What would you want them to say? Write it all down.

3     Put First Things First !

It’s all about learning to prioritize and manage your time so that your things come first not last. your fears and be storong. This habit can also help you learn to overcome your fears and be strong during hard moments.

4     Think Win-Win !

Sonner or later, we realise that we not only owe a lot to the society we live in, we also understand that our seccess depends on others success. Life is not zero sum game. When we make it a habit to see how others also can benefit from our actions, from our decisions, we are better off for that, The world is too connected for us to think win:lose.

5     Seek First to Understand,
Then to Be Understood !

Even when you are arguing with your opponentsm it is a good form to first understand their arguments and try to express them even better than they can, It is even more important when we are interacting with our friends, colleagues and other collaborators. Liseten, understand, and only then, open your mouth.

6     Synergize !

Synergy changes the conventional math. 2 and 2 is not 4, but 5 or 6 ro 7. The whole is more than the sum of its parts. Teamwork. If you want to go fast, go alone. If you want to go far, teamup.

7     Sharpen the Saw !

Abraham Lincolm said: Give me six hours to chop down a tree and I will spend the first hour sharpening the axe. Your axe is you body, mind and soul.
