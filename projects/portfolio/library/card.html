This form can be used by adult residents of Indian River County. Others can find more information about how to register for a library card in person at one of our locations. A Florida driver’s license or ID number is required to process your library card application online in order to verify your identity.

Your library card will be mailed to you within two weeks. Once you receive your library card in the mail it will be in a temporary status while we conduct the verification process, which will take two weeks from the date you submit the library card application. While in this temporary status you will have the opportunity to check out up to five items. If your library card application cannot be processed you will be notified.

Responsibility Statement *

I am 18 years or older and hereby apply for the privilege to check out materials from the Orange County Library System. I agree to comply with all regulations, pay all fines, pay for the loss or damage of materials charged to this card, and give immediate notice of an address change or the loss of my card. A fee will be charged to replace a lost card. I understand that I am responsible for all materials checked out from the System on this card and that my card and borrowing privileges are not transferable. Delinquent accounts may be handed over to a collection agency.
