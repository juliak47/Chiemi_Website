Tab 2

Music in the Library: Nina Swope Dec 09 | 2-3pm

Enjoy a live performance of holiday standards and classical selections by professional violinist Nina Swope.

Enjoy a live performance by professional violinist Nina Swope. Hear holiday standards and classical selections. Hailing from Chisinau, Republic of Moldova, Nina and her family immigrated to America in 1992 - shortly after the collapse of the USSR. Nina is an acoustic and electric violinist in the Orlando area. Nina's past strenuous classical music tutelage does not hold her back from being extraordinarily versatile. She has collaborated with Sofia Carson, OneRepublic, STYX, Bernadette Peters, Ramin Djawadi and many others. She has been featured by ClassicFM, Yamaha Music USA, Yamaha Music EUROPE, and a few more for her violin covers of popular songs. She is currently working on her EP. To learn more about Nina Swope please visit ninaviolinist.com.
